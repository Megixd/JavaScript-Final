Help & Support

Find answers to the most common questions about our shop

What is an FAQ section?

An FAQ section can be used to quickly answer common questions about your business like "Where do you ship to?", "What are your opening hours?", or "How can I book a service?".

Why do FAQ matter?

FAQs are a great way to help site visitors find quick answers to common questions about your business and create a better navigation experience.

Where can i add my FAQs

FAQs can be added to any page on your site or to your Wix mobile app, giving access to members on the go.

How do i add new questions & answers?

To add a new FAQ follow these steps: Manage FAQs from your site dashboard or in the Editor. Add a new question & answer. Assign your FAQ to a category Save and publish.

How do i edit or remove the FAQ?

You can edit the title from the FAQ 'Settings' tab in the Editor. To remove the title from your mobile app go to the 'Site & App' tab in your Owner's app and customize.
